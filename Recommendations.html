Here is a list of my top 5 best motorcycles for beginning riders.

  1. Honda Rebel 300/ 500
    The Rebel boasts a mighty package in a small bike. These lightweight cruisers provide riders an approachable seat height and a managable fist motorcycle
  2. Yamaha MT-03
    This entry level naked sportbike provides miles of fun in a reasobably priced packkage.
  3. Honda CBR500R
    The CBR500R is a solid middleweight bike that can offer a new rider a bike with room to grow or a returning rider a solid platform to get re-familiar with riding.
  4. Yamaha XT250
    This is an aweome little dual sport (meaning it can go on and off road) with an approachable seat height for those not virtically gifted.
  5. Kawasaki Z125 Pro
    This is Kawasaki's answer to the Honda Grom. This fun small displacement bike tries hard not to take itself too seriously.
